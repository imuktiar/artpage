About Me

When I was in preschool, my favorite time of day was when I was allowed to draw and paint. As I got older, this love never faded and eventually I went to college to study art. I am currently at UCF majoring in Studio Art.I use many different mediums, such as lino prints, acrylic, oil, charcoal and copic markers. I believe that working in a single medium is restrictive to an artistic process. Each idea manifests in its own individual style. I enjoy the beauty of living in the moment and many of my pieces are directly influenced by these moments I try to capture. I love recreating plants and animals in my work.
